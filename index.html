About Me

Junior UI/UX Designer

Hey, I'm Valeriia!

Originally from Ukraine, currently based in Greater Manchester. With a strong love for art and creativity, I've found my passion in UI and UX Design, focusing on visual website design. I enjoy crafting clean, modern, and engaging interfaces that are both beautiful and functional.

While I specialize in visual design, I also apply key UX principles to ensure intuitive, user-friendly experiences. I primarily work in Figma, using it to design responsive websites, build wireframes, and create interactive prototypes that bring ideas to life. I'm always learning and inspired by the connection between people and digital design.
